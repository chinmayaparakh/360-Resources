About us

360 Resources is a Multipurpose website that provides Study material for all students from classes 9 and above up to Bachelor level, helps you locate places such as restaurants and help you locate your nearby daily helpers like plumbers, electrians etc.

The founders and creators of 360 Resources are Aayush Mittal, Arsh Thakur and Chinmaya Parakh of Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida and they are currently pursuing BTech in CSE in JIIT Noida.

We have made this website particularly for students who leave their homes for their studies and have to live in hostels or PGs who are in constant need of such resources.
